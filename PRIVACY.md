# Privacy Policy — WC Shorts

_Last updated: 2026-06-13_

WC Shorts ("the App") is a personal, automated tool that creates short-form
highlight videos from football matches and publishes them to the operator's own
social media accounts (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram).

## 1. Who operates this App
This App is operated privately by its developer for use with **their own**
connected accounts. It is not offered as a service to third parties.

## 2. What data the App accesses
When you (the account owner) authorize the App, it uses platform APIs solely to:
- read basic account information needed to authenticate (e.g. your user id);
- upload and publish video content **to your own account**.

The App does **not** read your private messages, your followers' data, or any
content belonging to other users.

## 3. What data the App stores
The App stores only what is required to operate autonomously, on the operator's
own machine:
- OAuth access/refresh **tokens** (encrypted at rest by the host OS), used to
  keep posting without repeated logins;
- identifiers of already-processed matches/events, to avoid duplicate posts.

No personal data is sold, shared, or transmitted to any third party beyond the
official platform APIs (YouTube, TikTok, Meta) required to publish content.

## 4. Data retention & deletion
Tokens and operational state live only on the operator's machine and can be
deleted at any time by removing the App's local `data/` directory and revoking
access in the respective platform's app settings.

## 5. Third-party platforms
Published content is subject to the privacy policies and terms of the platform
it is posted to (YouTube, TikTok, Instagram). Please review their policies.

## 6. Contact
For questions about this policy, contact the operator via the repository:
